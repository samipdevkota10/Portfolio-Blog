MailLens was built to make large email datasets explorable through natural language instead of brittle filters.

## What I built

- Semantic search over unstructured campaign content
- Grounded question answering backed by retrieved dataset context
- Extraction pipelines for campaign patterns, promotions, and content trends

## Why it matters

The interesting part was not just adding vector search. It was combining retrieval quality, API design, and dataset structure into something analysts could actually use.
